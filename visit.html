Welcome to Wonderlab

At Wonderlab we invite you on a journey of exploration and discovery in the fascinating world of science. Our museum is a hub of knowledge, curiosity and innovation where visitors of all ages can engage with the wonders of the universe.

Practical Information

Wonderlab is located at:

Oxford St, London W1B 3AG, United Kingdom

The entrance is free for all

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 Vbucks per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
